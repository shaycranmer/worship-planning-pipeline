PRAYER_OF_DAY: Patient God, you scatter your word with open hands, onto path and
rock and thorn and soil alike. Make of us good ground: unhurried, undefended,
deep enough to hold what you plant. Give us the long faith of growing things,
that what takes root in us may feed more people than we will ever meet. **Amen.**

PRAYERS_OF_INTERCESSION: Rooted in God's patience, we pray for the church, the
world, and all who wait for good soil.

For every congregation learning to grow slowly; for planters and tenders of
small things, that they are not discouraged by unseen beginnings. God of the
seed, **hear our prayer.**

For the earth itself: for farmland and rain in due season, for the waters of
this region and all who keep them, that we may be tenders and not takers. God
of the seed, **hear our prayer.**

For all whose growing is interrupted: the grieving, the sick, the uprooted, and
those known to our hearts alone. God of the seed, **hear our prayer.**

Into your patient hands, O God, we place these prayers, trusting the harvest to
you alone. **Amen.**

BLESSING: May the Sower go out ahead of you, may good soil find your feet, and
may what is planted in you this day grow taller than your fear. **Amen.**

DISMISSAL: Go in peace. Grow where you are planted.
**Thanks be to God.**
